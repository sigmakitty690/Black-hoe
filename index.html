EVENT HORIZON

The boundary of no return. Inside, escape velocity exceeds c; the silhouette we see is the black hole's "shadow".

PHOTON RING

Light trapped in unstable orbits at ≈ 1.5 rₛ, lensed into a razor-thin luminous halo around the shadow.

ACCRETION DISK

Superheated gas spiralling inward, radiating across X-ray to radio wavelengths as it loses angular momentum.

04
